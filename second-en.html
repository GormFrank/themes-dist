Jobs & the workplace

Find work, explore skills and training, make career decisions, plan retirement and apply for temporary financial assistance.

Follow:

  • YouTube
  • Twitter
  • Flickr

Find a job

Find public and private sector job opportunities or hiring programs, apply or extend a work permit, get a Social Insurance Number, a criminal record check or security clearance.

Explore careers

Explore career options, search occupations by Canadian city to find available jobs, learn about average wages, and job requirements.

Training

Information on education, training, financial assistance and the skills needed to find and keep a job.

For employers

Information and tools to help find, hire, train, retain workers and includes how to advertise job openings.

Self-employment

Information and tools to help you plan and start a business and, includes how to name, register and finance a business, hire and pay employees.

Workplace standards

Information about federal and provincial labour laws, workplace standards, federal health and safety standards and labour relations programs.

Pensions & retirement

Information about employer and personal pensions, federal benefits and registered retirement savings plans and tools to help you plan for retirement.

Employment Insurance

Information about the Employment Insurance (EI) and related programs, includes how to apply for EI online, submit a report, and instructions for employers on how to submit a worker's Record of Employment.

Subscribe to Job Alerts

Job Alerts is a free email service that notifies you about locally available jobs that match your skills.

Getting ready for a flight?

Before you fly, learn more about what to pack and be prepared for the security screening.

Travelling Abroad?

Find the latest passport application forms online. Simply fill out, print, and submit!
